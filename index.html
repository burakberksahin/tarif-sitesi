Italian Meatballs

There’s nothing more comforting than a steaming bowl of meatballs in marinara sauce served over spaghetti noodles, and coupled with a side of garlic bread, it was the perfect meal that I always daydreamed about.

For 4 People |

Preparation Time: 1 Hour |

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

Bread crumbs: Use store-bought bread crumbs or make your own at home.

Milk: Milk adds moisture, ensuring the meatballs don’t dry out during the cooking process.

Onions: An onion lends bold flavor.

Meat: You’ll need a pound of ground beef and a pound of ground pork.

Preperation Steps

Cook the onion.

Mix all the ingredients together. Cove and chill.

Form the mixture into balls.

Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven.
